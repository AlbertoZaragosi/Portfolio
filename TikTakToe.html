Tik Tak Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe, a classic game also known as Noughts and Crosses. This Java-based application allows a solo player to challenge their strategic thinking against an artificial intelligence opponent.

Players take turns with the AI, making their moves through the console. The program then evaluates the moves, determines the winner or declares a draw, and presents the current state of the board.
